Support Resources

    You cannot open a ticket by sending email to support@informatica.com. That email address only works for tickets that are already open, and the ticket number is on the subject line.

    To open a ticket:

    • Use the Web-based Support portal at https://network.informatica.com/welcome.

      • For help in using the portal to open a ticket, see:
        https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=G4etwK_s-4M.

      • 日本語の説明

      The portal is the preferred method, as it allows you to easily attach log files, packet captures, etc.

    • Alternatively, you can call the Support hot line. For instructions:
      https://www.informatica.com/services-and-training/customer-success-services/contact-us.html

    • If you are having trouble getting through, you can send email to dlmessagingsuptcc@informatica.com. However, this is often not the most reliable way to contact Support.

    For tips on opening a problem ticket that can be resolved quickly, see
    https://ultramessaging.github.io/currdoc/doc/Operations/contactinginformaticasupport.html

    Full information on Informatica Support can be found at
    https://network.informatica.com/docs/DOC-3015

The master source for Informatica Ultra Messaging resources on GitHub are at
https://github.com/UltraMessaging
